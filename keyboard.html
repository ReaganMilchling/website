Keyboard

For years I've wanted to try an alternative keyboard layout. I looked at the more expensive ergodox or moonlander but decided to try and build one myself last August.

I settled on a lily58 because it seemed the most similar to your standard 100% or TKL. My hope was that I would still be able to switch back to a normal keyboard. Apart from regularly turning on caps lock, I have no issues working from the office without my keyboard. The lily58 has completely replaced my previous Keychron.
